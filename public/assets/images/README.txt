To replace the images in your app, simply upload your own images to this folder and name them exactly as follows:

1. hero.jpg (The main background image)
2. gallery-1.jpg (Living Area)
3. gallery-2.jpg (Ocean View Bedroom)
4. gallery-3.jpg (Modern Kitchen)
5. gallery-4.jpg (Oceanfront Terrace)
6. gallery-5.jpg (Master Suite)

The app is already configured to look for these filenames in this folder.
